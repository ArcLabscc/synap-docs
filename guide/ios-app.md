# Synap iOS App

The Synap iOS app gives you a native interface to your knowledge graph with 3D visualization, voice capture, and Apple Watch companion.

## Features

- **3D Brain Graph** — See your thoughts as a spatial network. Tap nodes to explore, pinch to zoom, fly to connections.
- **Voice Capture** — Record thoughts by voice, transcribed and tagged automatically.
- **Chat** — AI assistant with full knowledge graph context, powered by Claude.
- **Apple Watch** — Capture thoughts from your wrist. Recent thoughts at a glance.
- **Widgets** — Home screen widgets showing recent thoughts and graph stats.

## Download

Available on the App Store: [Synap](https://apps.apple.com/app/synap)

## Setup

1. Open Synap and tap **Log In**
2. Enter your email and password (same account as `npx arx-setup`)
3. Your knowledge graph loads automatically

All thoughts captured in the app appear in your AI tool sessions, and vice versa. Same account, same graph, everywhere.

## Driving Mode

The app detects when you're driving and enables voice-only capture mode. Thoughts are queued and synced when you're back online.

## HealthKit Integration

Synap optionally reads HealthKit data (steps, sleep, HRV) to enrich your knowledge graph with wellness context. Your AI can reference this data to provide more contextual responses.

::: info
The iOS app requires iOS 17.0 or later.
:::
